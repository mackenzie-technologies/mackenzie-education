G.C.E. Ordinary Level

Intensive Syllabus Mastery for High Distinction

Course Highlights

A comprehensive preparation for the O/L English Literature examination.

  • Poetry Anthology: In-depth line-by-line analysis of all prescribed poems.
  • Novels & Drama: Critical study of 'The Vendor of Sweets' and 'Twilight of a Crane'.
  • Answer Writing: Training on structuring answers to meet marking scheme criteria.
  • Past Papers: Extensive drilling of past 10 years' papers.

Success Strategy

We focus on "Context, Content, and Criticism" to ensure students can write sophisticated answers that stand out to examiners.
