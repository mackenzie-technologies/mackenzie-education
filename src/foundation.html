Curriculum Overview

Our Foundation program is designed to transition students from basic language acquisition to literary appreciation. We focus on:

  • Mechanics of English: Advanced grammar, sentence structure, and vocabulary building.
  • Creative Expression: Encouraging students to write their own stories and poems.
  • 2026 Reforms Alignment: Preparing students for the upcoming curriculum changes.
  • Early Literary Appreciation: Introduction to classic and modern short stories and poetry.

Why Start Early?

Mastering literature takes time. By starting in Grade 6 relative to O/L, students develop the critical thinking skills necessary for high grades effortlessly.
