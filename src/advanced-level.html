G.C.E. Advanced Level

University-Style Critical Analysis for Future Scholars

Syllabus Coverage

Designed for Arts and Law stream students, focusing on critical theory and deep textual analysis.

  • Shakespeare: Detailed study of Othello/Macbeth with focus on themes and soliloquies.
  • Modern Prose: Analyzing narrative techniques in Joyce, Hemingway, and others.
  • Sri Lankan Literature: Understanding the post-colonial context in local poetry and prose.
  • Critical Writing: Moving beyond summary to University-level critique.

University Preparation

This course bridges the gap between A/L and First Year University, using academic journals and critical essays as supplementary reading.
