Oxford Preparation Classes

Oxford AQA English Classes for advanced language skills and qualifications

Curriculum Overview

Our Oxford AQA English preparation develops critical language skills vital for high-level International qualifications. We emphasize academic argumentation and communication:

  • Critical Reading & Analysis: Strong focus on literary non-fiction, source-based reading, and deep textual inference.
  • Directed Composition: Crafting and adapting sophisticated writing for distinct purposes and audiences.
  • Organization & Synthesis: Elevating the ability to synthesize complex ideas logically and compellingly.
  • Non-exam Strategies: Expert support for non-exam assessment (NEA) elements promoting university-level research skills.

Stepping Stone to University

We build a robust foundation in linguistic frameworks and independent study methods that perfectly mirror the expectations of top-tier universities worldwide.
